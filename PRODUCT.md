# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Jenny and friends who need a quick way to move song links between Apple Music and Spotify without opening several tabs or doing manual search.

## Product Purpose

This tool exists to make music-link handoff feel instant: choose the direction, paste a song URL, get the matching destination URL or a useful search fallback, then copy or open it with minimal friction. Success means the page feels trustworthy, fast, and pleasant enough to keep around as a tiny everyday utility.

## Brand Personality

Pink, playful, slick. The interface should feel like a polished web tool with a little personal sparkle, not a heavy music-service clone.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS dashboards, dark music-app mimicry, plain developer demos, and anything that feels like a marketing landing page instead of a working tool.

## Design Principles

- Lead with the task: paste, convert, copy.
- Keep the interface friendly, but do not let decoration slow the workflow.
- Use playful color with product-grade clarity and contrast.
- Make success and fallback states obvious, especially when a direct Spotify match is unavailable.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Aim for WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-operable controls, visible focus states, reduced-motion-safe transitions, and status text that communicates success or errors without relying on color alone.
